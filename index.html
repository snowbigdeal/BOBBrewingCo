Our Story

In 1848, the same year as the Seneca Falls Convention, 5 unique characters came together and hatched a genius plan that grew to become the Blackout Bear Brewing Co. we know and love today. Early one morning Mike, Lee, Amanda and Marj were traveling in a wagon toward the capital of New York State. Their wagon hit a rock and broke the axel just outside of Bear City, NY. Seemingly out of nowhere, Alex appeared. He offered to fix the wagon at no cost to the traveling party. But before he could even begin work, an extremely intoxicated bear stumbled out of the woods. The bear looked at them with a great hunger in his eyes, but instead, hiccuped and wandered away. Our 5 survivors found the nearest pub and regaled the locals with their tale. Everyone loved their story so much, they decided to stay in Bear City forever. Since all of them liked to drink (except Alex, because he was too young), and didn’t have any other marketable skills, they started brewing beer. They crafted each brew in honor of the bear that spared them that fateful day.
